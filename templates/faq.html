Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about downloading X (Twitter) videos with SaveLinkX.

General

Is it free to download X (Twitter) videos?

Yes, SaveLinkX is completely free. No account, subscription, or payment is required.

Do I need to install any software or extension?

No. SaveLinkX works 100% in your browser - no apps, extensions, or plugins needed. Just paste the link and go.

How do I get the link of a tweet with a video?

On X/Twitter, tap the three-dot menu on the tweet and select "Copy link to post". You can also copy the URL directly from your browser's address bar.

Compatibility

Does it work on iPhone and Android?

Yes. SaveLinkX works in any mobile browser, including Safari (iOS) and Chrome (Android). Paste the tweet link and the video will be saved directly to your device.

How do I download an X video on iPhone?

Open Safari on your iPhone, go to savelinkx.com, paste the tweet link, and tap Download Now. The MP4 will be saved to your Files app or photo library.

Can I download animated GIFs from X (Twitter)?

Yes. Twitter GIFs are treated as videos on the platform and can be downloaded normally in MP4 format.

Does it work with X Spaces videos?

SaveLinkX is optimized for videos in public tweets. Support for X Spaces may vary depending on whether the recording is attached to the tweet.

Quality & Formats

What video formats are supported?

Videos are downloaded in MP4, compatible with all phones, tablets, and computers. When available, WebM and M4A options are also offered.

Can I download X (Twitter) videos in HD?

Yes. When available, SaveLinkX offers multiple quality options including HD and Full HD. Choose your resolution from the menu before downloading.

Privacy & Security

Does SaveLinkX store my videos?

No. Videos are processed and sent directly to your device. We do not store any files on our servers after the download completes.

Troubleshooting

Why can't some videos be downloaded?

Videos from private or protected accounts cannot be downloaded. Only public tweets with video are supported. Archived live streams may also be unavailable.

Is it legal to download X (Twitter) videos?

You should only download videos you own or have explicit permission to save. Always respect copyright law and X's Terms of Service.
